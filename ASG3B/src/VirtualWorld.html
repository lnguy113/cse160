Sanrio Story

Welcome to my Sanrio Café! Located the heart of a quaint Japanese-style café, nestled amidst the delicate petals of cherry blossoms, the soft murmur of laughter filled the air as characters settled at a table adorned with cherry blossom-themed decor. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee wafted through the air, mingling with the sweet scent of cherry blossoms, creating an ambiance of tranquility and warmth. Characters come to share tales of their adventures and cherished memories over steaming cups of café, enveloped in the serenity of the cherry blossom-filled surroundings.

As the server here, you have a special ability. By simply clicking the key "c", you can magically place a steaming cup of coffee for our dear customers to enjoy. Watch as their faces light up with delight at the sight of their favorite brew!

But wait, what's this? Once our lovely customers have savored every last drop of their coffee, it's time for you to work your magic again. With a quick tap of the key "v", you can whisk away the empty cup, leaving the table clean and ready for the next round of delicious treats.

So go ahead, take a sip of that virtual coffee and get ready to serve up smiles in our enchanting Sanrio Café!

Meet the Characters

Hello Kitty: With her signature bow and bright-eyed charm, Hello Kitty is the epitome of sweetness and innocence. Always ready with a warm smile and always waving at friends, making everyone feel welcome and loved.

Melody: My Melody is a gentle and caring soul, known for her soft pink hood and sweet demeanor. With her kind heart and nurturing nature, she is the friend everyone turns to in times of need, offering comfort and support with her soothing presence.

Kuromi: Kuromi is a mischievous yet lovable character, often sporting her punk-inspired attire and devilish grin. Despite her rebellious exterior, she has a heart of gold and fiercely loyal to her friends. With her playful antics and witty sense of humor, she adds a spark of excitement to any gathering.

Koroppi: Koroppi is an adorable frog with a love for nature and adventure. With his green overalls and friendly smile, he embodies the spirit of curiosity and exploration. Always eager to discover new places and make new friends, he brings a sense of wonder and joy to the world around him.
